Redwood Suite

The Redwood Suite is a modern Loft in Thessaloniki, which can accommodate up to 4 people. Its Minimal aesthetic based on the philosophy of Wood offers the right choice for a relaxing and pleasant stay in the city. It is ideally located near points of interest and just 10 minutes from the center of Thessaloniki:
Airport (11km), White Tower (2.6km), War Museum of Thessaloniki (1.8km), Hippocrates Hospital (550m), Thessaloniki City Hall (2km), Mediterranean Cosmos (7km), Aristotelous Square (3.5km).
(There is a bus stop nearby).
