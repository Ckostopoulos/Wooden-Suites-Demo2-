About Us

A Luxury Loft
with Nature

Welcome to the WOODEN SUITES, the luxury lofts of Thessaloniki. Combining elegance and good quality, our Suites offer an unparalleled and an unforgettable stay in the city of Thessaloniki. Four stunning and modern lofts are there to accommodate you and make yourself at home! Whether you are seeking for a business trip or looking for a few relaxing days in the city, the wooden suites provide the perfect retreat for every occasion. Just a few minutes from the center of Thessaloniki, guests can enjoy what this city has to offer while being comfortable and luxurious.
