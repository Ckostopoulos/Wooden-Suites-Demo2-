Enjoy the Greek Culture in the middle of the charming city of Thessaloniki.

From the old times, the rich history and culture of Thessaloniki echoes off the coast of Thermaikos. From the White Tower, the Arch of Galerius and the Rotunda, the Church of Agios Dimitrios and the bustling Aristotelous Square, the city of Thessaloniki offers a variety of attractions worth visiting. In Thessaloniki you can do everything. From activities to shopping. The city also stands out for its nightlife and food and it is certain that everyone will have an unforgettable time during their stay.
